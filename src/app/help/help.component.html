The BRAVO! dashboard enables employees and managers to recognize fellow employees for outstanding work achievements.

There are two types of BRAVO! recognition:

1. “On-the-Spot” recognition
  • Use BRAVO! cards to provide on-the-spot recognition to employees for their efforts and contributions when the employee has gone beyond the call of duty and performed a particular outstanding project, product, action, or reflection of our values.
2. Formal recognition
  • Recognize an employee or work group for a significant contribution or prolonged excellence in one of the following seven (7) elements: Safety, Quality, Delivery, Cost, Morale, Environment and or/Community involvement.

BRAVO! Features:

  • Managers and employees can nominate fellow employees for recognition.
  • Employees can recognize colleagues from any site or department in USA/CAN.
  • There are two separate nomination forms available for either ‘on the spot recognition’ or ‘formal recognition’.
  • Once a nomination of either recognition is submitted, a notification will be send to submitter as acknowledgement.
  • Time-based reports can be generated by location to analyze recognition trends (weekly, monthly).
